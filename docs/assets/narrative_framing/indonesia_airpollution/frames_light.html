Transportation Emissions

Air pollution caused by emissions from motor vehicles (cars, motorcycles, trucks, buses), including fuel quality, traffic, and related road use. Includes references to private and public transport, and road dust resuspension due to traffic.

Industrial Emissions

Air pollution caused by emissions from factories, manufacturing, industrial processes, and mining operations. Includes references to pabrik, industri, and related production activities.

Power Plant Emissions

Air pollution caused by emissions from coal-fired power plants and other fossil-fueled electricity generation facilities, including PLTU and related terms.

Biomass Burning and Agriculture

Air pollution caused by burning of biomass for land clearing, slash-and-burn agriculture, deforestation, and wildfires. Includes references to pembakaran lahan, kebakaran hutan, and related agricultural burning.

Household Emissions

Air pollution caused by household use of fossil fuels or biomass for cooking and heating. Includes references to pembakaran rumah tangga, penggunaan kayu bakar, and related terms.

Waste Management Emissions

Air pollution caused by open burning of waste, including municipal solid waste, landfill fires, and neighborhood trash burning. Only applies when combustion is explicitly mentioned.

Construction and Dust

Air pollution caused by construction activities, roadworks, and resuspended soil or dust, especially in urban settings.

Natural and Weather Factors

Non-anthropogenic or weather-related contributors to air pollution, such as prolonged dry season, low rainfall, or meteorological conditions that exacerbate pollution.
