Transportation-related Air Pollution

Air pollution caused by emissions from vehicles such as cars, motorcycles, buses, trucks, and other forms of motorized transport, including references to traffic, road dust resuspension, and fuel combustion in the transport sector.

Industrial and Manufacturing Emissions

Air pollution caused by emissions from factories, manufacturing facilities, industrial fuel combustion, and mining operations, including references to smelters, steel, cement, and other heavy industry.

Power Plant and Energy Generation Emissions

Air pollution caused by emissions from coal-fired power plants and other fossil-fueled electricity generation facilities, including references to PLTU, pembangkit listrik, and long-range transport of emissions from these sources.

Biomass Burning and Agricultural Fires

Air pollution caused by burning of biomass for land clearing, slash-and-burn agriculture, deforestation, and wildfires, including references to pembakaran lahan, kebakaran hutan, and agricultural burning.

Household Fuel and Cooking Emissions

Air pollution caused by household use of fossil fuels or biomass for cooking and heating, including references to pembakaran rumah tangga, kompor kayu, and penggunaan bahan bakar padat di rumah.

Waste Burning and Open Disposal

Air pollution caused by open burning of waste, including municipal solid waste, landfill fires, and neighborhood trash burning. Only applies when combustion of waste is explicitly mentioned.

Construction and Dust-related Pollution

Air pollution caused by construction activities, roadworks, and resuspended soil or dust, including references to debu konstruksi, pembangunan, and road dust.

Natural and Meteorological Factors

Non-anthropogenic factors that influence air pollution levels, such as low rainfall, El Nino, or seasonal weather patterns, but not direct emission sources.
