Factory Farming and Industrial Production

Coverage focusing on large-scale, intensive livestock systems, their efficiency, scale, and associated social or ecological consequences, excluding animal sentience or climate unless explicitly tied to production methods.

Animal Suffering and Sentience

Coverage centered on the moral or emotional harm to animals, empathy, or recognition of animals as sentient beings, focusing on pain, rights, or welfare.

Plant-Based and Alternative Proteins

Coverage promoting or assessing plant-based, cultivated, or other alternative proteins as substitutes for meat, often linked to innovation, ethics, or sustainability, but not general diet advice.

Environmental Impacts

Coverage linking meat production to climate change, deforestation, land use, water, or biodiversity, including both critical and defensive narratives, but not focused on production methods or animal welfare unless explicitly tied to ecological outcomes.

Human Health Effects

Coverage about health risks or benefits of meat consumption, such as cancer, cholesterol, obesity, or nutritional debates, focusing on consumer health rather than public health threats from production.

Epidemic and Antimicrobial-Resistance Risks

Coverage linking intensive farming or meat supply chains to zoonotic disease, pandemic preparedness, or antibiotic overuse, focusing on public-health and One-Health reasoning.
